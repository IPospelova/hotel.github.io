Spa

With design inspired by traditional local embroidery and fabrics, this calming space is dominated by light blue colour. Intentionally sub-lit, with diffused, indirect lighting to sustain a sense of relaxation, it offers the latest in natural massage and experiences, while using organic herbs from the island. The pool area and the juice bar nearby are just two of the highlights that await you.

  

Restaurant & Bars

Adopting a modern approach to authentic Mediterranean fare, the Royal Myconian Resort’s two fine restaurants and three bars offer a range of inspired selections that emphasize purity, simplicity, authentic Mykonos specialties and seasonal flavors in a stylish ambiance with stunning views, creating a truly unique culinary experience.

Pool

Long leisurely strolls in the gentle surf and lazy days splashing around the pool’s shimmering turquoise waters will refresh and rejuvenate you - body and soul. • Large seawater pool (700m2) with Jacuzzi, lily-pad designer sun loungers, day beds • Sun terraces



Beach

The hotel is situated directly on a 300m of beach. Curl up with the kids under a cabana by the sea, watch the most amazing sunset in Crete and relax. Beach Free facilities include: Beach towels, Showers & changing cabins, Umbrellas & sun beds with soft mattresses.
